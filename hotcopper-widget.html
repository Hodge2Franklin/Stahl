Will the widgets affect page load times?

No, our widgets are designed to load asynchronously and have minimal impact on page load times. The initial JavaScript snippet is lightweight (under 10KB) and the widget content is loaded only after the main page content has loaded. We also use lazy loading techniques to ensure the widgets only load when they're about to enter the viewport. Our testing shows an average impact of less than 100ms on total page load time.

Our targeting system analyzes the content of each discussion thread to determine relevance. We look at stock tickers mentioned, discussion topics, sentiment, and user engagement patterns.
